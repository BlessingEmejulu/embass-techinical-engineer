About Embass Tech

Embass Tech is a trusted, family-run engineering business based in Nnewi, Anambra State, Nigeria.

For nearly 30 years, our company has provided high-quality water borehole drilling and plumbing services across Nigeria, completing thousands of projects ranging from residential homes to institutions like schools, churches, hospitals, and farms.
